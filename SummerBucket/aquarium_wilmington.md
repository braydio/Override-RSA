# Wilmington Aquarium Visit

The North Carolina Aquarium at Fort Fisher, located near Wilmington in Kure Beach, focuses on the waters of the Cape Fear region. Highlights include:

- **Cape Fear Conservatory** – a tree-filled atrium with freshwater habitats for fish, amphibians, and reptiles.
- **Marine Exhibits** – showcasing local marine life such as alligators and sea turtles.
- **Albino Alligator Exhibit** – opened in 2009.
- **Venomous Snakes Display** – featuring rattlesnakes, copperheads, and cottonmouths.

The aquarium is regularly listed among the top attractions in North Carolina. Nearby points of interest include Fort Fisher State Historic Site and the beaches of Pleasure Island.
